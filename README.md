      * CHAP. II. I do prosecute the religion they haue, and of the Idols they do worship.

      * CHAP. III. How little they doo esteeme their Idols whome they worshippe.

      * CHAP VI. How they hold for a certaintie, that the soule is immotall, and that he shal haue another life: in the which it shalbe punished or rewarded according vnto the workes which he doth in this world, and how they pray for the dead.
